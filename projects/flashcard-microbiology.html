Flashcard Microbiology

About

Flashcard Microbiology is an essential resource designed for first and second year Medical students studying microbiology. PA students, Pharmacy students and Nursing students will find it useful as well. How Flashcard Microbiology is different from other study resources is that the user can test their microbiology knowledge in multiple ways. In Test mode, users can select one or multiple classes of organisms and viruses. From there users can test their knowledge on various characteristics of drugs such as Mechanism of Action, Adverse Reactions, Kinetics, etc.
