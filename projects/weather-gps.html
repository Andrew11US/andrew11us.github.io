Weather GPS

About

Don't let bad weather take you by surprise!
WeatherGPS will provide you with current weather conditions and forecasts for next 10 days in your city.
Prepare for your day with accurate forecasts.
App has stunning blur design with beautiful transparency and beautiful blur background.
In addition to current weather conditions and temperature you also have barometer info, cloudiness, wind speed, humidity and UV Index.
To update weather info just shake your iPhone.
Perfectly works on all devices with iOS 13 and up support both iPhone & iPad.

Features
Swift
Alamofire
Barometer build-in
iPad support
M1 support
