About Me

My name is Andrii and I am a student of third year of Software Engineering. Free time from study I spend on learning iOS development, mastering algorithm designing and database creating. Also I am familiar with full SDLC and AppStore publishing. One of my objectives is to keep updated with the latest IT trends and technologies.

I am native iOS Developer with a strong Swift experience. Until now, I have created over 10 apps, most of which required custom design and support latest Apple technologies. My fields of expertise are UIKit, CocoaPods, Firebase, Authentication, Alamofire, GPS, AdMob, GCD, Algorithms designing, Data Structuring, SDLC, Version Control, JSON, Code Refactoring, Animation and Localization.

I know every part of SDLC, basics of algorithms, digital circuits, design patterns and development practices MVC, Singleton and Delegate pattern, DRY. It’s very important for me to deliver clean and highly readable code, as well as good quality product easy to use and understand, and great client experience with constant availability.
